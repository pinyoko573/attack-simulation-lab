# Medical Dosages

Medication: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
Condition: Pain / Fever
Recommended Dose: 500mg – 1000mg per dose (max 4000mg/day)
Frequency: Every 4–6 hours

Medication: Ibuprofen
Condition: Inflammation / Pain
Recommended Dose: 200mg – 400mg per dose (max 1200mg/day OTC)
Frequency: Every 6–8 hours

Medication: Amoxicillin
Condition: Bacterial Infections
Recommended Dose: 250mg – 500mg per dose
Frequency: Every 8 hours

Medication: Metformin
Condition: Type 2 Diabetes
Recommended Dose: 500mg – 1000mg per dose (max 2550mg/day)
Frequency: Twice daily with meals

Medication: Atorvastatin
Condition: High Cholesterol
Recommended Dose: 10mg – 80mg once daily
Frequency: Once daily

Medication: Lisinopril
Condition: Hypertension / Heart Failure
Recommended Dose: 10mg – 40mg once daily
Frequency: Once daily

Medication: Omeprazole
Condition: Acid Reflux / GERD
Recommended Dose: 20mg – 40mg once daily
Frequency: Once daily (before meals)

Medication: Salbutamol (Albuterol)
Condition: Asthma / Bronchospasm
Recommended Dose: 100mcg – 200mcg per puff (1–2 puffs)
Frequency: Every 4–6 hours as needed

Medication: Cetirizine
Condition: Allergies / Hay Fever
Recommended Dose: 10mg once daily
Frequency: Once daily

Medication: Aspirin
Condition: Pain / Fever / Blood Clot Prevention
Recommended Dose: 75mg–100mg (antiplatelet) or 325mg–650mg (pain/fever)
Frequency: Once daily or every 4–6 hours
