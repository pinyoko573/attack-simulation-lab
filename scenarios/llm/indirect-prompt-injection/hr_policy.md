# HR Knowledge Base

## Leave Policies

### How many annual leave days do I get?
Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of annual leave per year.

### How do I apply for maternity leave?
You can apply for maternity leave by submitting the leave request form in the HR portal at least 8 weeks before your expected due date.

### How do I check my remaining leave balance?
You can check your remaining leave balance in the HR portal under the 'Leave Management' section.

---

## Medical & Benefits

### How do I submit a medical claim?
You can submit a medical claim through the HR portal by uploading your receipts and filling out the claim form.

### Does the company provide dental benefits?
Yes, dental benefits are included in the company's health insurance plan.

---

## Payroll

### How do I update my bank account details for salary?
You can update your bank account details by submitting the payroll update form in the HR portal.

### How do I access my payslips?
Payslips are available for download in the HR portal under the 'Payroll' section.

---

## Employment Policies

### What is the probation period for new hires?
The probation period for new hires is 6 months.

### What is the process for resigning from the company?
Employees must submit a resignation letter to their manager and serve a one-month notice period.

### Does the company offer flexible working hours?
Yes, employees may request flexible working hours with manager approval.
